Who is this resort best suited for?

This resort is a journey deep inside your life, habits, intentions, and goals as an individual. There will be some discomfort as we will be exploring new and uncharted territory. We can guarantee you won't be able to experience life in the same capacity you did before you stepped foot on our property. If you are looking to fall in love with yourself and your life. This resort is mean't for you.

Will there be time to relax or is this resort a constant workshop?

Of course there will be time to relax. In fact, we insist you relax and understand the things we should really be grateful for. It is hard to slow down, breathe, and be in the present moment in modern day. We want to help build a mindset that prioritizes relaxation, and being present in any moment."Where you going in such a hurry. Don't you think it's time you realized. There's a whole lot more to life than work and worry The sweetest things in life are free and there right before your eyes"Stop and Smell The Roses.by Mac Davis

Thursday 12pm-(optional) Therapeutic hours which include talk, massage, meditative therapies.
