About Intentional Consumption Resort

Here our goal is to help you realize the true power of your mind. We aim to educate our guests on the importance of consumption whether that be consumption through eating habits, media intake, economically, and energetically. We offer a series of courses over a 5 day period. Each day is dedicated to the different types of consumption. This is not a time to judge. This is a time to reflect. We hope to help you realize how important it is to live a life YOU are proud of. Most tangible things you can get back but lost time is gone forever. We believe being intentional in each and every moment is a life well spent. The time you have should fulfill you.
